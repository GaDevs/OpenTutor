# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Focus on technical issues and evidence
- Accept feedback gracefully

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination
- Doxxing or sharing private data
- Disruptive trolling or bad-faith interactions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior violates this code.
